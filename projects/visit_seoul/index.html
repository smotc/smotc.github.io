SEOUL, SOUTH KOREA TOP 10 EXPERIENCES

 

GYEONGBOKBUNG PALACE National Folk Museum

This historical palace was the center of Korean royalty during the Joseon dynasty and was built in 1395 in the new capital city of Seoul.

Read More

Dongdaemun Design Plaza (DDP)

This futuristic building is an architectural landmark that resembles a spaceship. It houses art exhibits, unique shops, and a museum featuring the history of Seoul.

Read More

N Seoul Tower and Locks of Love

This iconic tower is actually a broadcasting tower, but it overlooks the city of Seoul and is a great spot to be photographed. It is also famous for the thousands of locks of love.

Read More

Shops of Insadong

This is like the Eureka Springs of Seoul. Lots of unique art and jewelry shops as well as small restaurants. You could spend all day here and find special gifts you wouldn't find anywhere else.

Read More

Seoul Furniture Museum

This strangely named museum is more than just a collection of ancient furniture. It is a glimpse into a way of life and the history of Seoul. The beautiful architecture of the complex is located up in the mountains overlooking the whole city of Seoul.

Read More

Korean War Memorial

It is a bit of a surprise, but this museum is definitely worth the visit. Dedicated to all those from nations around the world who lost their lives during the Korean War. It has a moving indoor tribute and history museum, while outside is a massive collection of every type of military vehicle and weapon imaginable, from tanks and planes to rocket launchers. Truly impressive and guaranteed to make you pause and think.

Read More

Gangnam

The Gangnam district is like a cross between Times Square and Beverly Hills Boulevard. Lots of lights, signs, and upscale shopping. It was made famous by the K-Pop songs "Gangnam Style".

Read More

Cat Cafe

Cat cafe's are a place to relax spending some time enjoying the company of cats. Some cat cafe's have exotic cat breeds that live on site permanently while others work with local pet adoption agencies to allow you to meet adoptable cats. You can enjoy tea or other drinks and have a small snack while visiting the cats.

Read More

Paris Baguette

Paris Baguette is a slice of Paris with a Korean twist. Usually pronounced "Pari Baga", you can find French style pastries with Asian style fillings. It is a beautiful sight to walk into a Paris Baguette.

Read More

Seoul Food

Honestly, the best reason to visit South Korea is the food. Kimchi, japchae, dolsot bibimbap,bulgogi, and more. It is a food lover's paradise.
